Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Finance Friend handles your data.

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Finance Friend ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal finance management application developed by Vinkal. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Finance Friend is designed with a privacy-first approach. We collect and store the following types of information:

  • Financial Data: Transaction records (income, expenses), budget settings, savings goals, bill reminders, subscription details, EMI calculations, net worth entries, and other financial data you manually enter into the App.
  • Preferences: App settings, theme preferences, dashboard customization, notification preferences, and currency settings.
  • Security Data: Your app lock PIN (stored locally in encrypted form).

3. How Your Data is Stored

All your personal and financial data is stored locally on your device. Finance Friend does NOT use any cloud servers, external databases, or third-party analytics. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

  • All financial records are stored in your device's local storage (browser localStorage / app storage).
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • No user accounts or login credentials are required.
  • We do not collect, track, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be transparent about what we do not do:

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identification.
  • We do NOT access your device contacts, camera, microphone, or location.
  • We do NOT collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or browser fingerprints.
  • We do NOT use any analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.).
  • We do NOT display any advertisements.
  • We do NOT sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.
  • We do NOT track your usage behavior or create user profiles.

5. Data Export & Backup

Finance Friend allows you to export your financial data as reports. When you chose to export data:

  • The exported file is saved directly to your device's download folder.
  • No data is uploaded to any server during the export process.
  • You are solely responsible for the security of exported files.

6. Local Notifications

Finance Friend may send local notifications for bill reminders, budget alerts, and monthly reports. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any server communication or push notification services.

7. Third-Party Services

Finance Friend does NOT integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The App operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection for any core functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Finance Friend is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special risks for children using the App. However, we recommend parental guidance for minors managing finances.

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All Data" option in the App Settings.
  • Clearing the App's data from your device's system settings.
  • Uninstalling the App (this permanently deletes all associated data).

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Optional PIN lock to prevent unauthorized access to the App.
  • All data stays on your device with no external transmission.
  • No cloud storage means no risk of server-side data breaches.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and on this page. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
